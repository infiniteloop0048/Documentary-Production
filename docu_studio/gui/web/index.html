Docu Studio v1.0

Automate your documentary pre-production

Generate scripts, voiceovers, and stock footage timelines — ready for your editor.

GUIDED MODE
You choose the topic

Enter your documentary topic and target duration. The app writes the script, records voiceover, sources footage, and exports a Filmora timeline.

FULL AUTO MODE
AI finds a trending topic

No input needed. The app searches for trending documentary subjects, picks the best one, and runs the entire pipeline automatically.

SHORT / REEL MODE
Vertical short-form video

Enter a topic and pick a length from 15 to 60 seconds. The app writes a hook-driven script, records voiceover, and assembles a 9:16 vertical cut with Ken Burns motion — ready for Shorts, Reels, or TikTok.

SLIDESHOW MODE
Turn images into a narrated video

Upload a set of images and write (or paste) a narration script. The app adds Ken Burns motion to each image, times it to your voiceover, and exports a finished video.

CLIP STORY MODE
Compile your own footage into a narrated video

Upload your own video clips in order, trim each one, and write (or flag for AI generation) narration for each — the app fits the narration to each clip's actual length and stitches them together.
